The Problem We're Solving

Your Excel Sheet Is Holding You Back

The current Quality Management Dashboard is a manual, fragmented, error-prone process. Sanchalak replaces it entirely — no more spreadsheets, no more chasing data, no more version confusion.

😤 Current State — Excel
  • ❌ Manual data entry into a shared Excel sheet every month — prone to errors and overwrites
  • ❌ No real-time visibility — you only know the status after someone updates the file
  • ❌ 46,000+ cumulative observations tracked in a single workbook — slow and fragile
  • ❌ Quality ratings, checklists, observations, and ageing data scattered across 8 different sheets
  • ❌ No structured form for field engineers — data comes in inconsistent formats
  • ❌ No access control — anyone can accidentally delete or corrupt data
  • ❌ Business leadership can't see live numbers without asking someone to share the file
  • ❌ Repetitive observation tracking done manually with no automation
✅ With Sanchalak
  • ✓ Field engineers fill structured digital forms — data flows directly into the system
  • ✓ Live dashboard — every observation, checklist, and rating updated in real time
  • ✓ All 46,000+ historical observations migrated and searchable in seconds
  • ✓ Single unified platform — no more jumping between sheets
  • ✓ Role-based access — field engineer, site incharge, and management views
  • ✓ Automatic ageing alerts — overdue observations surface without anyone chasing
  • ✓ Leadership sees live project-wise quality scores, closure rates, and trends anytime
  • ✓ Repetitive observations auto-flagged and tracked across months
Platform Features

What Sanchalak Does

Every module in Sanchalak maps directly to a sheet or process in your current Excel dashboard — but smarter, faster, and always live.

📋

Digital Observation Forms

Field engineers log observations, severity, contractor, stage, and target date directly from mobile or desktop. No paper, no WhatsApp, no Excel — structured data from the source.

Replaces Manual Entry
📊

Live Quality Ratings Dashboard

Project-wise and wing-wise quality scores calculated automatically from submitted data. Tracks ratings against the 8.5 target. Trending up or down — visible at a glance.

Replaces Obs Backup Sheet
🔔

Ageing & Overdue Alerts

Automatic flags for observations open beyond 7, 15, and 30 days. No more manually checking Ageing Analysis. The system surfaces what needs attention before it escalates.

Replaces Ageing Tracking
✅

BIM 360 Checklist Tracker

Track IP, R1, R2, and Closed checklists per project digitally. Monitor closure rates, open issues, and contractor-wise pending items — all in one view.

Replaces Checklist Sheet
🧱

Material Inspection Reports

Log material inspection records (Accepted / Rejected) per project and month. Track rejected material reasons, concrete quality reports, and inspection counts digitally.

Replaces MIR Section
🔁

Repetitive Observation Tracker

System automatically identifies observations that repeat across weeks or months for the same contractor or location — highlights patterns so the team can intervene early.

Replaces Repetitive Obs Sheet
🏗️

Cost Centre Management

Manage all projects — La Familia, Lavie, Verdant Vistas, Social Housing — as separate cost centres with individual dashboards, incharge assignments, and observation counts.

Replaces Cost Centre Sheet
📈

Business Analytics for Leadership

Translate quality numbers into business language — cost of rework, contractor performance scores, closure rates, severity breakdown. Built for management reviews, not just site teams.

New — Not in Excel
👥

Role-Based Access

Field Engineer → logs observations. Site Incharge → reviews and closes. Quality Manager → sees all projects. Management → sees live summary. Everyone sees only what they need.

New — Not in Excel
